Quick and Easy Recipe

Chocotorta

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of chocolate cookies
  • 400 g (14 oz) dulce de leche
  • 400 g (14 oz) cream cheese
  • Coffee or milk to dip the cookies
  • Cocoa powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix dulce de leche with cream cheese until smooth.
  2. Dip cookies in coffee or milk and layer in a dish.
  3. Spread a layer of cream mixture on top of the cookies.
  4. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  5. Sprinkle cocoa powder and refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
